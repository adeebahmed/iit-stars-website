I Wanna Be A Millionaire (Financial Trading Workshop)

Spring 2015

"I wanna be a millionaire...!" is a workshop to introduce middle and high school students to financial trading. Students will learn about stocks, the stock market, and howt to develop a small portfolio. Students will be able to "invest" in the stock market and observe performance of their portfolio.

IIT Computer Discovery Camp

Summer

This two week summer camp is perfect for any student. First time students learn to build LEGO mindstorm robots and then program them to do a variety of tasks from following a line to finding its way out of a maze. Returning students are given a chance to try their hand at new challenges, such as building a web page or app.

Advanced Lego Mindstorms After School Camp

Spring

This camp meets on Fridays after school in the Spring. It is open to both boys and girls with prior experience in LEGO Mindstorms. Students get a chance to work with LEGO mindstorm robots and work through more advanced challenges.

IIT Computer Discovery Club

Fall & Spring

Throughout the academic year, STARS at the Illinois Institute of Technology works diligently to promote computing to students in our community by means of a Saturday program. During this Saturday program we have taught students to program in Scratch, Python, HTML, and even Java. As our students evolve so does our program. The students have fun learning to think creatively while they program in practical ways.

IIT Fundamentals of Electronics Club

Spring

During this Saturday program, we guide students to the knowledge of electronic signals, circuits, hardware, and the use of a fundamental programming language while creating fun and creative projects using the Arduino Uno.
