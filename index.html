About Us

The goal of Stars Computing Corps at the Illinois Institute of Technology is to broaden participation of Middle School Children in computing.

We approach this goal by teaching the students how to build robots, playing games to inspire them, and encouraging them to constantly challenge themselves.

As college students, we know the importance of learning about technology, as early as possible. We not only acts as teachers, but we also strive to mentor all whom we meet throughout the program.
