About the Camp

The Advanced Lego Mindstorms After School Camp was started in Spring 2014. This program is open to middle school boys and girls, who have prior experience with Lego Mindstorms. Students get a chance to build Lego Mindstorm robots and tackle more challenging problems including math, logic and Custom routines.

Duration: Four Consecutive Fridays

Technology: Lego Mindstorms NXT 2.0

Grade Level: Open to middle school girls and boys entering grades 7-9 with prior experience in Lego Mindstorms.
