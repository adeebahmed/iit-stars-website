About the Camp

The IIT Computer Discovery Camp was first held in summer of 2010 and was funded by an NSF Broadening Participation in Computing (BPC) grant obtained by Prof. Cindy Hood (Dept. of Computer Science, IIT). The focus of the camp was to encourage middle school girls to learn and become interested in computers. The camp is organized and run by Prof. Vida Winans (Dept. of Computer Science, IIT). The camp was again offered in the summer of 2011 and was partially funded by generous donations of IIT alumni.

Duration: Two weeks

Technology: First Time students get a chance to work with LEGO mindstorm robots, learning how to build them and then program the robots to do a variety of tasks, from following a line to finding its way out of a maze. Returning students also are given a chance to try their hand at new challenges, such as building a web page or app.

Grade Level: Open to middle school girls entereing grades 7-9 or returning camp students of all grades.
